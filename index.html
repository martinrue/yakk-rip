Here lies Yakk

An app that helped over 500 people learn Esperanto and Vietnamese.

And now, the good news:

Yakk has resurrected as LangSesh – a bigger and better language learning app!

I got questions!
Why did Yakk shut down?
Yakk's mission was to help people discover the joy of learning languages, but new content for Yakk was very expensive and slow to make. We wanted to help more people learn more languages faster.

So, we went back to the drawing board and came up with something new – something that solved that problem.

We came up with LangSesh.
What is LangSesh?
LangSesh is a brand new app, created to help learners build strong conversational ability from the start. No useless sentences, like a certain Owl app.

LangSesh is better, faster and cheaper.

From official courses like A1/A2, to dating in a foreign language, LangSesh has something for everyone. Not to mention achievements and progress tracking.
Why should I use LangSesh?
LangSesh is aimed at people who value their time and want to realy learn how to speak their target language.

While LangSesh tracks your progress and even has achievements, we'll never let it get in the way of real learning and progress.

LangSesh is so cheap that some people paid with the change they found in their couches. But don't go digging just yet, you get 10 free lessons and don't even need to sign up.
